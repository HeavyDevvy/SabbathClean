# Berry Events - Complete Home Services Platform Workflow

## Project Overview
Transform Berry Events into a comprehensive home services marketplace with end-to-end booking capabilities for cleaning, plumbing, electrical, chef services, moving, and au pair services. Build a seamless platform with location-based booking, calendar integration, and secure payment processing.

## Core Service Categories & Implementation

### 1. CLEANING SERVICES
**Service Types:**
- Regular House Cleaning (Weekly/Bi-weekly/Monthly)
- Deep Cleaning (Spring cleaning, move-in/move-out)
- Office Cleaning (Daily/Weekly/Monthly contracts)
- Carpet & Upholstery Cleaning
- Window Cleaning (Interior/Exterior)
- Post-Construction Cleaning
- Event Cleanup Services

**Booking Flow for Cleaning:**
```
Step 1: Service Selection
- Choose cleaning type
- Select property size (1-2 bed, 3-4 bed, 5+ bed, office space)
- Frequency selection (one-time, weekly, bi-weekly, monthly)

Step 2: Location & Property Details
- GPS-based address input with map confirmation
- Property type (house, apartment, office, retail)
- Special requirements (pets, specific areas, supplies needed)
- Access instructions and key information

Step 3: Schedule Selection
- Calendar widget showing available slots
- Time preference (morning, afternoon, evening)
- Recurring schedule setup for regular cleaning

Step 4: Service Customization
- Add-on services (inside oven, fridge, cabinets)
- Supply preference (bring own supplies vs use client's)
- Special instructions and preferences

Step 5: Provider Selection & Confirmation
- View matched cleaners with ratings, photos, reviews
- Compare pricing and availability
- Select preferred cleaner
- Review booking details and confirm
```
